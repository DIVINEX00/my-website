✨ About Us

Welcome to Divinex Eatries — an exquisite dining sanctuary where culinary artistry meets
timeless elegance.

Nestled in the heart of Owerri, Divinex Eatries was born from a vision
to redefine fine dining. Since our grand opening in 2025, we have set out to offer our
distinguished guests not merely a meal,but an experience — an unforgettable symphony
of flavor, ambiance, and impeccable service.

Guided by a passion for perfection, our chefs
craft each dish as a masterpiece, blending contemporary techniques with the finest
seasonal ingredients. Signature creations like the amazing Costecoglou showcase our
dedication to innovation while honoring the classic traditions that inspire us.

Our dining
room, adorned with warm lighting, rich textures, and bespoke details, creates an atmosphere
of refined sophistication — the perfect backdrop for intimate dinners, celebrations, and
gatherings that deserve nothing but the exceptional.

At Divinex Eatries, luxury is not an
indulgence, but a promise: that every moment you spend with us will be distinguished by
care, artistry, and a commitment to excellence.

We invite you to step into a world where
elegance is effortless, and each experience is crafted to delight the senses.
